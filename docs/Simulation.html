# Q-Learning Logic Update
Q(s, a) = Q(s, a) + α [ R + γ max Q(s', a') - Q(s, a) ]

Why Q-Learning?

  • 1. It adapts without manual rule updates.
  • 2. It handles "Noise" (micro movements) effectively.
  • 3. Off policy learning allows to optimize for maximum security stability.

Jitter (Micro-shaking)

The variance in velocity between telemetry packets. Humans have unique muscular noise; bots are too smooth.

Dwell Time (Rhythm)

The duration a key is held. This biometric signal is highly stable for specific users across different devices.

Quantitative Metrics

Velocity

Pixels / Second

Human acceleration follows a logarithmic curve. Bots move linearly. Velocity helps us spot inhuman precision.

Jitter

Variance in Velocity

Humans have unique "micro shakes." Jitter measures the stability of movement between telemetry points.

Dwell Time

Key Depress Duration

The milliseconds a key remains held. Your unique rhythm is the spacing between these hold events.
